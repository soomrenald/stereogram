Viewing tip

Look through the image as if focusing behind the screen. For outward mode, try slightly crossing your eyes. The two red dots at the bottom are spaced one tile width apart and can help you gauge how much to diverge or cross your eyes. Small changes in tile width and depth strength can make the hidden form easier to see.
